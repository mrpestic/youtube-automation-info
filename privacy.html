Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how YouTube Control ("we", "our", or "the application") handles your information when you use our automated YouTube upload service.

2. Information We Collect

When you authorize our application, we collect and process:

  • YouTube Account Access: Permission to upload videos to your YouTube channels
  • Video Metadata: Video titles, descriptions, tags, and upload settings
  • Authentication Tokens: OAuth 2.0 tokens to maintain authorized access

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Uploading videos to your authorized YouTube channels
  • Managing scheduled content publication
  • Maintaining authenticated access to perform authorized operations

4. Data Storage and Security

• All authentication tokens are stored locally and encrypted
• We do not store your videos on our servers
• We do not share your data with any third parties
• Access credentials are never transmitted to unauthorized parties

5. Data Retention

Authentication tokens are retained only as long as necessary to provide the service. You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

6. Third-Party Services

This application uses the YouTube Data API v3 provided by Google. Your use of YouTube services is subject to the YouTube Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke application access at any time
  • Request deletion of stored authentication data
  • Access information about what data we process

8. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
