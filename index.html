YouTube Control

About

YouTube Control is a personal automation tool designed for scheduled content management and video uploads to YouTube channels. This application helps streamline the process of managing multiple YouTube channels efficiently.

Features

• Automated video uploads
• Scheduled content publishing
• Multi-channel management
• Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication

Data & Privacy

This application only accesses your YouTube channel data for the purpose of uploading and managing videos. All authentication tokens are stored securely and never shared with third parties.

For detailed information, please read our Privacy Policy.
